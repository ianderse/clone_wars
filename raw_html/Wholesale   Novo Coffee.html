Wholesale

*Wholesale log-in coming soon!*

When we roast our coffees, it’s an effort to do justice to the hard work of many others who invested time and energy into the beans before they arrived here in Denver. We view our wholesale customers as an extension of that process, and an opportunity to further showcase extraordinary coffees from producers around the world.

We roast green coffee beans in our beautiful vintage Vittoria machines, and we are proud to be able to offer you some of the most distinctive coffees available in today’s marketplace. We value our relationships with wholesale customers and understand every one is different, so we offer tailored training, equipment, and coffee programs to meet your needs. If you are interested in learning more about Novo’s wholesale program, we’d love to meet with you and talk about a potential partnership. Please fill out the inquiry form below and we’ll be in touch with you soon.
