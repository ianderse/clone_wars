About

Founded in 2002 by Jake, Herb, and Joseph Brodsky, we consider all of our employees and partners to be part of the Novo family. Our team is small, but each of us is passionate about our role and we look forward to coming to work every single day.

Equally as important, we love meeting and getting to know the folks who enjoy sipping our coffee. We offer educational coffee cuppings–another word for “tastings”–to the public, and if you’d like to come visit the roastery in person, please feel free to stop by on a Friday. We’re open to the public from 1:00–4:00 pm, or you can give us a call anytime at (303) 295-7678. We also serve up delicious coffee, pastries, and whole beans at our retail location at 1600 Glenarm Place. We hope to see you soon!

Learn about our process

Coffee is commonplace, yet a truly great cup reveals a complex process. We roast coffees early in the week in our vintage Vittoria machines (fun fact: one of them is powered by a Ferrari motor). We deliver the coffees to some of Colorado’s finest independent restaurants, coffee shops, and natural grocers promptly after roasting. We believe coffee is more than a commodity, and we are always striving for constant improvement in search of a delicious cup. While we take coffee seriously, we firmly believe in not taking ourselves too seriously, as evidenced by impromptu ping pong tournaments, plasma cars, and a fridge full of local microbrews.
